Robert Schonefeld

Founder & CEO

Ian Stuart

Director of Loan Originations & Investor Relations - Southern Region

Based in San Diego, Ian Stuart brings 30 years of banking and commercial real estate lending experience to Anderson Roberts, where he leads loan originations and investor relations for the Southern region.
